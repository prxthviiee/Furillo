About Us

At Furillo, we believe every animal deserves a safe, loving home. Our mission is to create a caring and connected space where people and organizations can come together to make a real difference in the lives of animals. Whether it's a lost pet that needs to find its way back home, a rescued animal in need of shelter, or a loving family searching to adopt — Furillo is here to help. We combine the power of technology with the compassion of a dedicated community. Individuals can quickly report lost or found pets, explore adoption opportunities, and reach out to trusted NGOs with just a few clicks. Animal welfare organizations can use our platform to spread awareness, manage rescue efforts, and connect with those eager to help. Furillo isn't just an app — it's a movement. A movement to ensure that no animal is left behind, and every act of kindness counts. Together, we're building a safer world for animals — one paw at a time.

Our Mission

Furillo is dedicated to helping lost pets reunite with their families, supporting pet adoption, and enabling rapid rescue for animals in need.

What We Offer

Lost & Found Pet Reporting

Quickly report or search for missing pets. Our system alerts nearby users and NGOs to help reunite animals with their families.

Pet Adoption Platform

Explore and connect with trusted adoption listings. Whether adopting or rehoming, Furillo ensures safe and heartfelt matches.

Rescue Alerts for Emergencies

Send instant alerts to local NGOs when an animal is in distress. Time-sensitive help reaches where it's needed, fast.

NGO Collaboration & Dashboards

Animal welfare groups can track rescue efforts, manage posts, and connect with users using powerful, easy-to-use dashboards.

Awareness Campaigns

NGOs can create and manage awareness campaigns to educate communities about animal welfare, adoption events, and rescue initiatives.

Modern, User-Friendly Experience

Clean design, fast performance, and mobile responsiveness ensure every user feels welcome—on any device.
